A half-breed from Mexico and USA, Francisco y Madero creates a distinct blend of latin sampledelia mixed with Californian pop/surf/psychedelia. San Franciscan Jess Sylvester (Violent Change, Marinero) and Mexican Carlos Pesina (Micropapitas, Los Amparito) met in Mexico through a Myspace message in 2010, and have been making music together since through recording sessions passed through Dropbox.
